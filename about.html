What is tanum.co? We are a new independent and sprouting company based in Miami Beach, founded in 2018, specializing in plant arrangements. We may not deal with your typical flower arrangements, but we can add a touch of foresty green to any spot. No project is too big or too small for us.

Every small detail in each arrangement is taken into consideration and every plant is handled with love and care. All of our plants are real and grown naturally. Our goal is to give our customers a beautiful and personalized experience with their new plant friends.

We have many options to choose from, from plant types, sizes, colors, etc. View our image gallery or visit our Instagram page to view our previous work. And feel free to contact us for more information.
